How to Use

  • To search for a player: Enter a name in the search bar or double click on a player node.
    • Note that you must type the first name and last name, and the spelling must match exactly with the spelling listed on Football Reference.
    • An edge between nodes means players are similar!
  • Hover your mouse over a node to see player information.
  • Scroll in/out on the graph to zoom in/out
  • Click and drag on the graph to pan.
  • Click and drag on nodes to rearrange/pin. Right click a node to unpin.
